Park Overview

Fun Adventure Amusement Park, established in the early 2020s, is located along the bustling A1 road, ensuring convenient access for visitors from all around the region. The park was created with the goal of providing endless entertainment and joyous experiences for families, children, and thrill-seekers alike. From exhilarating rides to tranquil spots for relaxation, Fun Adventure Amusement Park offers something for everyone.

The park's mission is to provide a safe, family-friendly environment where visitors can make lasting memories. Whether you're looking to have a fun-filled day of adventure or simply want a relaxing escape, we offer a wide range of attractions to satisfy all your desires. We pride ourselves in our commitment to excellence, ensuring that every visitor leaves with a smile.

Location and Directions

Address:
Plot 34256 Along the A1 road, Phakalane, Botswana
Directions:
From Gaborone, take the A1 road heading north. Turn left into Phakalane, continue straight, and then take a right turn after the circle. The park entrance will be on your left. It’s a straightforward route, ensuring ease of travel for all visitors.
Public Transportation:
Public transport is easily accessible with combies passing right next to the park. You can hop on a combi that travels towards Phakalane, and you'll find yourself just a short walk away from the entrance. For your convenience, the area is well-served by various local bus services.
Parking:
We offer ample parking space for both small and large vehicles. Parking is free for all visitors, ensuring that your arrival is as smooth and hassle-free as possible.

Hours of Operation

General Hours:
Monday - Friday: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Saturday & Sunday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Special Hours:
During holidays and peak seasons, the park may extend its hours to accommodate the increased number of visitors. Stay updated with our schedule through our website or by calling the park directly.
Holiday Schedule:
On national holidays and during peak vacation periods, the park is often open for extended hours to give you more time to enjoy everything we have to offer. Be sure to check our events calendar for updates on holiday hours.

Exciting Activities for All Ages

At Fun Adventure Amusement Park, we offer a diverse range of activities tailored to every age group and interest. Whether you're an adrenaline junkie looking for thrilling rides or someone who prefers a slower pace, our park has something for everyone.

Thrilling Rides

The park is home to a variety of heart-racing rides, including roller coasters, bumper cars, and spinning teacups. The ‘Thunder Roller Coaster’ is a favorite among thrill-seekers, offering high-speed twists and turns that are sure to get your heart racing.

Gentle Rides and Attractions

For those looking for something more laid-back, our gentle rides such as the Ferris wheel and carousel are perfect. These classic attractions are designed for the whole family to enjoy, offering breathtaking views of the park.

Water Fun

Beat the heat with our water-themed attractions. The ‘Splashdown’ water slide and the ‘Water Kingdom’ pool area provide hours of wet and wild fun. Families can enjoy relaxing in the lazy river or get drenched on the water-based rides.

Live Entertainment

Every weekend, the park features live entertainment, including performances by local musicians, magicians, and dancers. These performances take place in the central plaza, so be sure to check out the schedule and catch a show during your visit.

Delicious Food and Dining

No adventure is complete without a delicious meal to refuel! At Fun Adventure Amusement Park, we offer a wide variety of dining options to satisfy every palate. From quick bites to sit-down meals, you’ll find something to suit your tastes.

Food Court

Our food court offers a range of options, including fast food favorites like burgers, fries, and pizza, as well as healthier options such as salads and fresh fruit. The food court is centrally located, so it’s easy to grab a bite between rides.

Café and Snack Stalls

For those in need of a lighter snack, we have several cafés and snack stalls scattered throughout the park. Whether you’re craving ice cream, cotton candy, or a fresh smoothie, you’ll find plenty of delicious treats to enjoy while you explore.

Special Events and Promotions

Throughout the year, we host a variety of special events and promotions to make your visit even more memorable. From seasonal festivals to themed days, there's always something exciting happening at Fun Adventure Amusement Park!

Annual Summer Festival

Our Summer Festival, held every year in July, is one of the park’s biggest events. It features live music, food trucks, special performances, and unique themed activities. It’s the perfect way to spend a summer day with friends and family.

Discounted Tickets

Keep an eye on our website for special promotions and discounted ticket offers. We often run seasonal promotions that allow you to experience all the fun at a lower price. Our “Family Day” deals are perfect for large groups looking to enjoy the park together.
